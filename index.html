Explore Nature and Complete Fun Outdoor Challenges

This website encourages you to explore the outdoors and connect with nature through various challenges and scenic locations.


Have you ever said something so terminally online, your replies were full of people telling you to “touch grass?” Were you at a total loss of how and where you could find said “grass” to touch? We’ve all been there, but with Go Touch Grass you can finally touch grass and provide proof of it with fun badges.

How does it work?

Each trail featured has its own page with basic information, as well as a video featuring highlights of the trail and an interactive 360 camera photoset, so you can get a good idea of what you will experience IRL ahead of time
Actually go outside! We want you to touch grass for real
After visiting the trail, head back online to our “Achievements” tab and check off every task you successfully completed to earn cool badges you can display on your profile Share your badges with friends online, so everyone knows you have actually touched grass
