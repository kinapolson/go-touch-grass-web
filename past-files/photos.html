Lake Eola Park

Lake Eola Rough Draft Lake Eola Loop is located in Downtown Orlando–roughly a twenty minute walk from UCF’s Downtown Campus. This 0.9 mile paved trail features aesthetics both natural and urban, making it an ideal starting point for first-time grass-touchers. FLORA AND FAUNA: ACTIVITIES: Lake Eola Loop has a rich history and culture.
