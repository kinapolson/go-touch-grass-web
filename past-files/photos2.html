Overview

Lake Eola is located in the heart of downtown Orlando and is one of the city's most iconic natural spaces.

The trail around the lake is just under a mile and offers scenic views of wildlife, art installations, and Orlando’s skyline.

The park has a long history dating back to the 1800s and remains a popular spot for recreation and community events.

Flora & Fauna

  • Swans (multiple species)
  • City ducks
  • Turtles
  • Fish and small birds
  • Flowering trees and lakeshore vegetation

Activities

  • Swan Boat Rentals
  • The "Muse of Discovery" Art Sculpture
  • Lake Eola Fountain
  • Sunday Farmer's Market
  • Walking and jogging loop
  • Eola House visitor center

Achievements

  • Eola Fauna – Observe wildlife
